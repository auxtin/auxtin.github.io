About

My decision to become an aspiring Mathematician was a happy accident. When I was an incoming first year, the orientation leader that helped me declare what major to become had to go off of my response to his question "What do you want to do?" -- to which I responded I want to work on computers and I like Math. Nearly one year later, thinking that I am just like the other Computer Science and Engineering students, I started to notice a diverence of the people I would see in my Computer Science classes taking Engineering classes, and even upper division CS classes while I was taking seemingly endless Math classes. Already having the passion for Math I cultivated in High School, I was okay with all the math classes, and even enjoyed them. This worked out for the best because a little bit later on, I realized I could have the best of both worlds by pursing Data Science. Currently I am honing my skills in Software Engineering, but learning what I can about Data Science.
